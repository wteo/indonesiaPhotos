About


Indonesia, a captivating South East Asian archipelago of over 17,000 islands, offers diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage. In 2018, I journeyed through this mesmerizing country, capturing its vibrant colors and unique beauty, from ancient temples and festivals to pristine beaches and lush rainforests.


Immersing myself in cultural history, traditional batik textiles, and enchanting Balinese dances and music, I also discovered awe-inspiring natural wonders like Bali's beaches, Raja Ampat's coral reefs, Sumatra's rainforests, and Java's volcanoes. These images, showcased here, reveal Indonesia's cultural richness and stunning landscapes.


Hover over the bottom-right corner of an image for a short description, and let these photos inspire you to explore Indonesia, experiencing its warm hospitality and captivating landscapes.


Embark on this visual journey through Indonesia's tropical paradise and let your imagination soar, as we uncover the allure of this South East Asian gem.
